Process

This website was developed as the official portfolio for Alex Ginanjar - an Indonesian photographer known for his landscape, human interest, and editorial work. The website aims to present Alex's photography in a clean digital format, making it easy for clients, collectors, and art enthusiasts to explored and connect with him directly.

Credit: Unsplash.

The main goal was to build a clean, visually-focused website that showcases Alex's high-resolution photos in their best form. The biggest challenge was ensuring fast loading speeds while displaying large image files, and designing an intuitive user experience across all devices.

The process begins with deeper dive into the style and design that suits Alex to determine the categories of photos to be displayed, the stories behind the work, and the booking features and contact form. The design concept is minimalistic, with generous white space to keep the photos as the hero element. Navigation is kept simple and smooth, with an immersive fullscreen gallery experience that lets visitors enjoy each detail distraction-free. A neutral color palette was chosen for a timeless look that complements Alex's visual style.
