The Athelier landing page was designed to introduce an exclusive collection of flower vases that blend timeless classic design with modern touches. This project aims to attract collectors and interior enthusiasts who appreciate artistic details.

Credit: Unsplash.

The main goal was to build an elegant, accessible page that highlights the unique story of Athelier vase. The biggest challenge was presenting rich, detailed product visuals while ensuring the browsing experience stays smooth and fast.

The process started with visual trend research, wireframing, and final development focused on responsive layout and strong visual storytelling. The design concept draws inspiration from European art galleries and antique ceramic shops. A color pallete of white, blue, black, and beige was chosen to emphasize a clean, timeless, and premium feel that elevates the artistic product photography.
